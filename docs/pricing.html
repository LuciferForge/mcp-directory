FAQ

Does paying for placement compromise the organic directory?

No. Sponsored slots are visually labelled (`Sponsored` / `Featured` tag) and live alongside the organic ranking, not replacing it. The organic ranking algorithm continues to use stars, recency, install count, and category fit.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes — 7-day money-back on monthly tiers if you're unsatisfied. Verified is a one-time fee and is non-refundable once the review is delivered.

What if my server isn't listed yet?

Submit it at /submit first. Once it's in the directory you can apply for a slot. Or apply now and mention it in the notes — we'll handle both in one review.

Do you accept crypto / international payments?

Yes. Polar.sh covers cards globally. We can also invoice via Stripe, Razorpay (INR), or accept stablecoins (USDC on Polygon) for annual plans.

Why is there no self-checkout?

Manual review keeps the directory high-signal — we won't take money to feature a broken or low-quality server. Average application-to-live time is under 48 hours.
